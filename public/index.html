Welcome to Fyreside!

Watch large twitch + youtube streams...
with a chat that you can actually interact with.

How does it work?

  1. Find your favourite stream on Twitch.tv or Youtube and enter its URL into Fyreside
  2. We'll randomly put you into a chatroom with 25 people or less.
  3. Watch the stream, meet new people, and have a conversation about the game.
